Phishbowl

Phishbowl is a local, offline social-engineering simulator built for red teamers. Step into the attacker role inside a sealed sandbox and practice realistic human hacking against AI-driven personas in three fake apps:

- Messenger
- Social feed
- Email client

Start with a briefing and an objective, then use pretexting, phishing, and manipulation to achieve your goal. Every persona is powered by a local Ollama LLM and carries its own personality, knowledge, secrets, and a suspicion meter that rises when you make mistakes.

Unlike defensive training tools that target employees, Phishbowl trains the attacker mindset: recon, pretext creation, rapport building, and adaptive pivoting — all with no real victims and nothing leaving your machine.
